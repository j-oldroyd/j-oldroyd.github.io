Theorem 10.4.8. Properties of the Cross Product.

Let \(\vv{a},\vv{b},\vv{c}\) be vectors and \(c\) a scalar. Then the following properties are true:

  1. \(\vv{a}\times\vv{b} = -\vv{b}\times\vv{a}\)
  2. \((c\vv{a})\times\vv{b} = c(\vv{a}\times\vv{b}) = \vv{a}\times(c\vv{b})\)
  3. \(\vv{a}\times(\vv{b}+\vv{c}) = \vv{a}\times\vv{b}+\vv{a}\times\vv{c}\)
  4. \((\vv{a}+\vv{b})\times\vv{c} = \vv{a}\times\vv{c}+\vv{b}\times\vv{c}\)
  5. \(\vv{a}\cdot(\vv{b}\times\vv{c}) = (\vv{a}\times\vv{b})\cdot\vv{c}\)
