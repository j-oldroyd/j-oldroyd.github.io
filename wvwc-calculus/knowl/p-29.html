It's straightforward to find the slope of a line: choose two distinct points \((x_{1}, y_{1})\) and \((x_{2}, y_{2})\) and the compute the rise over run:

\begin{equation*} m = \frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x} = \frac{y_{2} - y_{1}}{x_{2} - x_{1}}\text{.} \end{equation*}

If the line corresponds to some changing physical quantity, then the slope gives us the rate of change of that quantity.
