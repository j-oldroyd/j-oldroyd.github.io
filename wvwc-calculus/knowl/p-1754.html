The surface integral above measures how \(\vb{F}\) flows across the surface \(S\) in the direction of \(\vb{n}\text{.}\) However, we will always have two possible choices for \(\vb{n}\text{.}\) If \(S\) is orientable, then we know we can specify a consistent choice of direction \(\vb{n}\text{.}\) We just need to make sure this is done before computing the integral.
