Remember that a function \(f\) of the variable \(x\) is just a rule that turns one number, \(x\text{,}\) into another number, \(f(x)\text{.}\) So the idea that the limit of a function is trying to express is what happens to the number \(f(x)\) (the output) as the number \(x\) (the input) approaches some particular value.
