Definition 11.3.2. The Dot Product.

Let \(\vv{x} = \dotprod{x_{1},y_{1},z_{1}}\) and \(\vv{y} = \dotprod{x_{2},y_{2},z_{2}}\text{.}\) The dot product of \(\vv{x}\) with \(\vv{y}\text{,}\) denoted \(\vv{x}\cdot\vv{y},\) is given by

\begin{equation*} \vv{x}\cdot\vv{y} = x_{1}x_{2}+y_{1}y_{2}+z_{1}z_{2}. \end{equation*}
