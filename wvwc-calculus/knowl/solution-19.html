Solution 2.1.5.1.

We know that the velocity should be \(13\text{,}\) but we'll try to find it again using our new formula. So the velocity should also be

\begin{align*} \lim_{h\to0}\frac{s(3+h)-s(3)}{h} & = \lim_{h\to0}\frac{3(9+6h+h^{2})-5(3+h)-12}{h} \\ & = \lim_{h\to0}\frac{13h+3h^{2}}{h} \\ & = \lim_{h\to0}(13+3h) \\ & = 13. \end{align*}
