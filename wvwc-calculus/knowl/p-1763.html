So far we've considered several different versions of the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus. There is the Fundamental Theorem of Line Integrals which applies to the gradient operator \(\grad\text{,}\) Green's Theorem which applies to the two-dimensional curl, Stokes' Theorem which applies to \(\curl\text{,}\) and also Green's Theorem for Flux Integrals which applies to the two-dimensional divergence. The only remaining generalization is the divergence theorem.
