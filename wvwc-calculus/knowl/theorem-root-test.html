Theorem 9.4.9. Root Test.

Let \(\sum a_{k}\) be an infinite series and let

\begin{equation*} r = \lim_{k\to\infty}\sqrt[k]{|a_{k}|}\text{.} \end{equation*}

Then

  1. If \(r \lt 1\) the series converges absolutely.

  2. If \(r \gt 1\) the series diverges.

  3. If \(r = 1\) the test fails.
