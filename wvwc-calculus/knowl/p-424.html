Now, if we add up the areas of these rectangles we get an approximation for the area under the graph. Call these areas \(A_{1},A_{2},A_{3}\text{.}\) So the area under the graph of \(y = x^{3}\) from \(x=0\) to \(x=1\) is about

\begin{equation*} A_{1} + A_{2} + A_{3} = \frac{4}{9}. \end{equation*}
