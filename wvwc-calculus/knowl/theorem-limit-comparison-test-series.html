Theorem 8.3.9. Limit Comparison Test.

Suppose that \(\sum a_{k}\) and \(\sum b_{k}\) are both series with positive terms, and suppose

\begin{equation*} L = \lim_{k\to\infty}\frac{a_{k}}{b_{k}} \end{equation*}

exists. Then

  1. if \(0 \lt L \lt \infty\text{,}\) then either both series converge or both series diverge.

  2. if \(L = 0\) and \(\sum b_{k}\) converges, then so does \(\sum a_{k}\text{.}\)

  3. if \(L = \infty\) and \(\sum b_{k}\) diverges, then so does \(\sum a_{k}\text{.}\)
