Integrals of the form \(\int_{a}^{b} f(t)e^{st}\,dt\) are important in engineering and physics. Here, the variable \(s\) represents some parameter (usually complex). Let \(f(t)\) be the piecewise function given by

\begin{equation*} f(t) = \begin{cases} e^{-t} \amp 0 \leq t \leq 1 \\ 2 \amp 1 < t \leq 5 \\ e^{4t} \amp 7\leq t \leq 10 \\ 0 \amp \text{otherwise.} \end{cases}\text{.} \end{equation*}

Find \(\int_{-5}^{8}f(t)e^{st}\,dt\text{.}\)
