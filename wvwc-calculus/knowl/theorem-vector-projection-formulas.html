Theorem 11.3.13. Vector Projection Formulas.

Let \(\vv{a}\) and \(\vv{b}\) denote vectors in \(\RR^{2}\) or \(\RR^{3}\text{.}\) Then the projection of \(\vv{a}\) onto \(\vv{b}\) is given by

\begin{equation*} \proj{\vv{b}}{\vv{a}} = \frac{\vv{a}\cdot\vv{b}}{\vv{b}\cdot\vv{b}}\vv{b}. \end{equation*}
