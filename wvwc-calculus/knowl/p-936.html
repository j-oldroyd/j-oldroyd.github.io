\begin{equation*} \sum_{k=0}^{\infty}\frac{(-1)^{2k+1}}{k+1} = - \sum_{k=0}^{\infty}\frac{1}{k+1}\text{,} \end{equation*}

which diverges by the comparison test. At \(x = 1\text{,}\) the series reduces to the alternating harmonic series which converges by ExampleÂ 8.4.3.
