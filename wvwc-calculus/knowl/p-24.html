From sine and cosine, we can also define four more basic trigonometric functions: secant, cosecant, tangent and cotangent. These are defined as follows:

\begin{align*} \sec\theta \amp= \frac{1}{\cos\theta}\\ \csc\theta \amp= \frac{1}{\sin\theta}\\ \tan\theta \amp= \frac{\sin\theta}{\cos\theta}\\ \cot\theta \amp= \frac{\cos\theta}{\sin\theta}\text{.} \end{align*}
