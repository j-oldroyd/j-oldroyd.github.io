First, recall that work (very roughly) represents the net effect of a force \(F\) acting on a mass over some displacement \(d\text{.}\) If the force is constant (in both magnitude and direction), then we can simply define the work \(W\) accomplished by a force \(F\) as a particle is displaced by \(d\) units by the formula

\begin{equation*} W = Fd. \end{equation*}
