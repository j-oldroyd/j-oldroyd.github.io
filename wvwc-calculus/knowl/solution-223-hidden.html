We have

\begin{align*} f_{xy} & = (f_{x})_{y} \\ & = (2m\cos(mx+ny)\sin(mx+ny))_{y} \\ & = -2mn\sin^{2}(mx+ny) + 2mn\cos^{2}(mx+ny) \\ & = 2mn\cos(2mx+2ny) \end{align*}

as well as

\begin{align*} f_{yx} & = (f_{y})_{x} \\ & = (2n\cos(mx+ny)\sin(mx+ny))_{x} \\ & = -2mn\sin^{2}(mx+ny) + 2mn\cos^{2}(mx+ny) \\ & = 2mn\cos(2mx+2ny) \end{align*}
