Using \(d = st\text{,}\) we have the following estimate for the displacement:

\begin{equation*} d = v(0)(6.4 - 0) + v(6.4)(12.2 - 6.4) + v(12.2)(15 - 12.2)\text{.} \end{equation*}

This is a left Riemann sum, and gives the estimate 62.94 m for \(d\text{.}\) The corresponding right Riemann sum gives the estimate 71.0 m instead.
