Finding magnitudes of vectors can also be done by applying the distance formula from SectionÂ 11.1 to the components of the vectors. The magnitude of a vector \(\vv{x}\) is denoted by \(|\vv{x}|\) or \(\norm{\vv{x}}\text{.}\) For example, the magnitude of \(\vv{b}-3\vv{a}\) from the previous example is
