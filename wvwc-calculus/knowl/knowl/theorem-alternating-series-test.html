Theorem 9.4.1. Alternating Series Test.

Consider the alternating series \(S = \sum (-1)^{k}a_{k}\text{,}\) where \(a_{k}\) is positive and decreasing. If \(a_{k}\to0\text{,}\) then the series converges. Furthermore, for such a series we have the remainder estimate

\begin{equation*} |R_{n}| = |S - S_{n}| \leq |a_{n+1}|\text{.} \end{equation*}
