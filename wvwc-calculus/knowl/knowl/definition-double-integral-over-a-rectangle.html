Definition 13.1.1. Double Integral Over a Rectangle.

Let \(f(x,y)\) be a function defined over the rectangle \(R\text{.}\) Then the double integral of \(f(x,y)\) over \(R\) is defined to be

\begin{equation*} \iint_{R}f(x,y)\,dA = \lim_{\Delta A\to0}\sum_{i=0}^{m}\sum_{j=0}^{n}f(x_{i},y_{j})\Delta A \end{equation*}

If the limit exists, we say that \(f\) is integrable over \(R\text{.}\)
