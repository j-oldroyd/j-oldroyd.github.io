Example 7.4.8. Another Laplace Transform.

The Laplace transform of a function \(f(t)\) is defined to be

\begin{equation*} \int_{0}^{\infty}f(t)e^{-st}\,st\text{,} \end{equation*}

assuming \(s\) is chosen so that the integral converges. Find the Laplace transform of \(f(t) = t\text{.}\)
