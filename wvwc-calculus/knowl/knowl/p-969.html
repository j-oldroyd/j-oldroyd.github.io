Now we need to check the endpoints. At \(x = -\frac{7}{8}\text{,}\) the series becomes \(\sum_{n = 1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}\text{,}\) which diverges by the \(p\)-series test. At \(x = -\frac{5}{8}\text{,}\) the series becomes \(\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{(-1)^{n}}{\sqrt{n}}\) which converges by the alternating series test.
