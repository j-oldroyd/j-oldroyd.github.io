Theorem 13.9.1. Divergence Theorem.

Let \(E\) be a simple solid region and let \(S\) be the boundary surface of \(E\) oriented outwards. Let \(\vb{F}\) denote a continuously differentiable vector field on \(E\text{.}\) Then

\begin{equation*} \iint_{S}\vb{F}\cdot d\vb{S} = \iiint_{E}\div\vb{F}\,dV\text{.} \end{equation*}
