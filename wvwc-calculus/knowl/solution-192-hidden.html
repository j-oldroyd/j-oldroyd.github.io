We'll find the radius of convergence first, which is given by

\begin{align*} R \amp = \lim_{k\to\infty}\frac{(2k + 2)!}{(2k)!} \\ \amp = \lim_{k\to\infty} (2k + 2)(2k + 1) \\ \amp = \infty \text{.} \end{align*}

So the radius of convergence is infinite, implying that the interval of convergence is \((-\infty,\infty)\text{.}\)
