First, assume \(\lim_{n\to\infty}x_{n} = x\text{.}\) Then taking the limit of both sides of the recurrence relation gives

\begin{equation*} x = \frac{1}{2}\left(x + \frac{2}{x}\right)\text{.} \end{equation*}

Solving for \(x\text{,}\) we get \(x^{2} = 2\text{,}\) which simplifies to \(x = \sqrt{2}\text{.}\)
