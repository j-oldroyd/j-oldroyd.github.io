Theorem 5.2.3. Mean Value Theorem.

Let \(f(x)\) be differentiable on some interval \([a,b]\text{.}\) Then there exists a number \(c\) in \((a,b)\) such that

\begin{equation*} f'(c) = \frac{f(b) - f(a)}{b-a}, \end{equation*}

or equivalently

\begin{equation*} f(b) - f(a) = f'(c)(b-a). \end{equation*}
