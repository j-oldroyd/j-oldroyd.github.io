Finally, at the critical point \((1,1)\) we have \(f(1,1) = 1\text{.}\) So putting this all together, we can say that the absolute maximum of \(f(x,y)\) on this triangle is \(4\text{,}\) and this is reached at \((4,0)\text{.}\) Similarly, the absolute minimum is \(0\text{,}\) and this is reached at the origin.
