Example 3.1.8. Tangent line of the sine function.

Find the equation of the tangent line to \(f(x) = \sin x\) at \(0\text{.}\)
Solution.
The tangent line must pass through \((0,0)\) and must have slope \(f'(0) = 1\text{,}\) so its equation is
\begin{equation*} y-0 = 1(x-0) \end{equation*}
or just \(y=x\text{.}\)
