Example 5.1.5. Another limit involving exponentials.

Find \(\lim_{t\to1^{+}}\frac{9^{t-1} - 4^{t-1}}{t-1}\text{.}\)
Solution.
This is a \(\frac{0}{0}\) indeterminate form, so L’Hospital’s Rule applies. We get
\begin{align*} \lim_{t\to1^{+}}\frac{9^{t-1} - 4^{t-1}}{t-1} & = \lim_{t\to1^{+}}\frac{9^{t-1}\ln9 - 4^{t-1}\ln4}{1} \\ & = \ln\frac{9}{4}. \end{align*}
