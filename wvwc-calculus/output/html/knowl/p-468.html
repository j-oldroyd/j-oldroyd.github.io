Let \(a,b\in\RR\) with \(a \lt b\) and let \(f(x)\) be a function defined on the interval \([a,b]\text{.}\) Let \(P = \{x_k\}_{k=0}^{n}\) be a partition of \([a,b]\) and choose corresponding sample points \(x_{k}^{*}\in[x_{k-1},x_{k}]\) for \(1\leq k\leq n\text{.}\) Then a Riemann sum is a sum of the form

\begin{equation*} \sum_{k=1}^{n}f(x_{k}^{*})\Delta x_{k} \end{equation*}

where \(\Delta x_k = x_{k} - x_{k-1}\text{.}\)
