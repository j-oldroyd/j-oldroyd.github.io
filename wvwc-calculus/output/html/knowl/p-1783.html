First, we parameterize the cone using
\begin{equation*} \vb{r}(x,y) = \dotprod{x, y, \sqrt{x^{2} + y^{2}}}, x^{2}\leq y\leq x\qq{and} 0\leq x\leq 1\text{.} \end{equation*}
This gives
\begin{equation*} \norm{\vb{r}_{x}\times\vb{r}_{y}} = 1\text{,} \end{equation*}
and so the area of the conical segment is
\begin{equation*} \int_{0}^{1}\int_{x^{2}}^{x}\,dy\,dx\text{.} \end{equation*}
