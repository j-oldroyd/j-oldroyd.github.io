Replacing the midpoints in DefinitionÂ 6.2.8 with corresponding left endpoints (respectively, right endpoints) gives the left endpoint rule (respectively, the right endpoint rule). Together with the midpoint rule, these approximations are

\begin{align*} L_n \amp = \sum_{k=1}^{n} f(x_{k-1})\Delta x\\ M_n \amp = \sum_{k=1}^{n}f\left(\frac{x_{k-1} + x_{k}}{2}\right)\Delta x \\ R_n \amp = \sum_{k=1}^{n} f(x_{k})\Delta x \text{.} \end{align*}

Careful! In general, \(M_n \neq \frac{L_n + R_n}{2}\text{.}\)
