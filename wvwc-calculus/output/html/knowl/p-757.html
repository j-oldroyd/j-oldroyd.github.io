The center of mass is just the moment divided by the total mass \(m\) of the system:
\begin{align*} \overline{x}\amp= \frac{M}{m}\\ \amp= \frac{80}{25+20+10}\\ \amp= \frac{80}{55}\\ \amp= \frac{16}{11}. \end{align*}
So the center of mass of the rod is at the point \(\overline{x} = \frac{16}{11}\text{.}\)
