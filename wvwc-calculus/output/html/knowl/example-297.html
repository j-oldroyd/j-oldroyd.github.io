Example 12.3.2.

Let \(f(x,y) = ye^{x-y} - x^{3}\tan y^{4}\text{.}\) Find \(\pdv{f}{y}\text{.}\)
Solution.
Since we need to find \(\pdv{f}{y}\text{,}\) we differentiate \(f(x,y)\) with respect to \(y\) and treat \(x\) as a constant:
\begin{align*} \pdv{f}{y} & = \pdv{}{y}\left[ye^{x-y} - x^{3}\tan y^{4}\right] \\ & = e^{x-y} - ye^{x-y} - 4x^{3}y^{3}\sec^{2}y^{4} \end{align*}
