Example 11.1.9. Spherical shells.

We can also use inequalities to describe regions in addition to equalities. For example, \(3^2\leq x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}\leq 4^2\) describes the region contained between the sphere of radius \(3\) and the sphere of radius \(4\text{,}\) both centered at the origin.
