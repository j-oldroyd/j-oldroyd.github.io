Example 3.2.2. Computing a derivative.

Compute the derivative of \(f(x) = x - 3x^{2}\text{.}\)
Solution.
Using DefinitionÂ 3.2.1, we have
\begin{align*} f'(x) & = \lim_{h\to0}\frac{[(x+h)-3(x+h)^{2}] - x + 3x^{2}}{h} \\ & = \lim_{h\to0}\frac{[x+h-3x^{2}-6xh-3h^{2}] - x + 3x^{2}}{h} \\ & = \lim_{h\to0}\frac{h-6xh-3h^{2}}{h} \\ & = \lim_{h\to0}(1-6x-3h) \\ & = 1-6x. \end{align*}
