Example 9.3.6. The Alternating Harmonic Series.

Explain why Theorem 9.3.3 cannot be applied to the alternating harmonic series
\begin{equation*} \sum_{k=1}^{\infty}\frac{(-1)^{k + 1}}{k}\text{.} \end{equation*}
Solution.
Since \(\frac{(-1)^{k+1}}{k}\) is neither decreasing nor positive, the Integral Test doesn’t apply here.
