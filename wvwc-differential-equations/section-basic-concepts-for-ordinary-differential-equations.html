First, a definition of the primary concept in this course: the differential equation.

Definition1.1.1Differential Equations

A differential equation is an equation relating some function with its derivatives. A differential equation that involves a function of only one independent variable is called an ordinary differential equation. A differential equation that involves a function of more than one independent variable (which you see a lot of in Calculus 3) is called a partial differential equation.
