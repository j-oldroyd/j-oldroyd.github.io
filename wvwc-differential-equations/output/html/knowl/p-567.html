Therefore a solution (but not the general solution!) of this ODE is given by
\begin{equation*} y = \sum_{n\geq0}c_{2n}x^{2n+\frac{1}{2}} = \sum_{n\geq0}\frac{(-1)^{n}}{(2n+1)!}c_{0}x^{2n+\frac{1}{2}}\text{,} \end{equation*}
which is actually just
\begin{equation*} y = c_{0}\frac{\sin x}{\sqrt{x}}\text{.} \end{equation*}
