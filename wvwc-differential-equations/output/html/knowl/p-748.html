The solution of the heat equation (6) satisfying the boundary conditions (7) and initial condition (8) is given by

\begin{equation} u(x,t) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}b_{n}\sin\frac{n\pi}{L}x e^{-\lambda^{2}_{n}t}\tag{10} \end{equation}

where

\begin{align} b_{n} \amp = \frac{2}{L}\int_{0}^{L}f(x)\sin\frac{n\pi}{L}x\,dx \tag{11}\\ \lambda_{n} \amp = c\frac{n\pi}{L}. \tag{12} \end{align}
