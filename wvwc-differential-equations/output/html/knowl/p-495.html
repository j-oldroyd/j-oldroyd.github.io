Therefore the general solution of the ODE is
\begin{align*} y &= \sum_{n\geq0}^{}c_{n}x^{n}\\ &= \sum_{n\geq0}c_{2n}x^{2n} + \sum_{n\geq0}c_{2n+1}x^{2n+1}\\ &= c_{0}\sum_{n\geq0}^{}(-1)^{n}\frac{x^{2n}}{2^{n}}+c_{1}\sum_{n\geq0}^{}(-1)^{n}\frac{x^{2n+1}}{2^{n}}\\ &= c_{0}\sum_{n\geq0}^{}\left(-\frac{x^{2}}{2}\right)^{n}+c_{1}x\sum_{n\geq0}^{}\left(-\frac{x^{2}}{2}\right)^{n}\\ &= \frac{c_{0}}{1+\frac{x^{2}}{2}} + \frac{c_{1}x}{1+\frac{x^{2}}{2}} \end{align*}
