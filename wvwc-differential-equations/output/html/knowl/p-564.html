To continue, we make the guess
\begin{equation*} y = x^{\frac{1}{2}}\sum_{n\geq0}^{}c_{n}x^{n} = \sum_{n\geq0}^{}c_{n}x^{n+\frac{1}{2}}\text{.} \end{equation*}
Now we plug this into the ODE to get
\begin{equation*} \sum_{n\geq0}c_{n}\parens{n+\frac{1}{2}}\parens{n-\frac{1}{2}}x^{n+\frac{1}{2}} + \sum_{n\geq0}c_{n}\parens{n+\frac{1}{2}}x^{n+\frac{1}{2}} + \sum_{n\geq0}c_{n}x^{n+\frac{5}{2}} - \sum_{n\geq0}\frac{1}{4}c_{n}x^{n+\frac{1}{2}} \end{equation*}
or just
\begin{equation*} \sum_{n\geq0}^{}\left[\parens{n+\frac{1}{2}}\parens{n-\frac{1}{2}}+\parens{n+\frac{1}{2}}-\frac{1}{4}\right]c_{n}x^{n+\frac{1}{2}} +\sum_{n\geq2}^{}c_{n-2}x^{n+\frac{1}{2}}=0\text{.} \end{equation*}
which simplifies to
\begin{equation*} \sum_{n\geq0} n(n+1)c_{n}x^{n+\frac{1}{2}}+\sum_{n\geq2}c_{n-2}x^{n+\frac{1}{2}} = 0\text{.} \end{equation*}
So the recurrence relation the coefficients \(c_{n}\) need to satisfy is
\begin{equation*} c_{n} = -\frac{1}{n(n+1)}c_{n-2}\quad\text{for}\quad n\geq2\text{.} \end{equation*}
