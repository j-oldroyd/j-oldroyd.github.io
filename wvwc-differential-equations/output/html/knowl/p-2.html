This document was created to serve as lecture notes for the Differential Equations course at West Virginia Wesleyan College. These notes are divided into two parts.
  • The first part, Ordinary Differential Equations, introduces differential equations in one variable along with methods for their solution and several applications.
  • The next part, Partial Differential Equations, introduces Fourier series and differential equations in several variables, building up to solving the heat and wave equations.
