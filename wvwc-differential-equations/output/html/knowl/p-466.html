Since this two-step recurrence relation is only valid for \(n\geq1\text{,}\) it places no restrictions on any constant terms of the series. We’ll need to determine these separately. To do so, write out the first couple terms of the sums in the previous equation:
\begin{equation*} c_{1}+2c_{2}x+\dots = -2c_{0}x -2c_{1}x^{2}+\cdots \end{equation*}
This tells us that \(c_{1} = 0\text{.}\) Again, we can’t get this information from the recurrence relation!
