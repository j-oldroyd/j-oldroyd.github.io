We can also consider approximating \(f\) with other trigonometric polynomials of degree \(N\text{,}\) say

\begin{equation*} F(x) = A_{0} + \sum_{k=1}^{N}[A_{k}\cos\frac{k\pi}{L}x + B_{k}\sin\frac{k\pi}{L}x]\text{.} \end{equation*}

We'd like to know how good the approximation is. To do this, we need to define a measure of error.
