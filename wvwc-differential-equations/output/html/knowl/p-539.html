After equating coefficients, our computations with Sage suggest that \(c_k + 2c_{k+2} = 0\text{,}\) or more simply
\begin{equation*} c_{k+2} = -\frac{1}{2}c_{k}\text{ for }k\geq0\text{.} \end{equation*}
This can of course be verified algebraically as we’ve done several times already, but we’ll trust Sage for now.
