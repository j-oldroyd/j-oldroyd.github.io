Theorem 2.27. Solution of Non-homogeneous Equations.

Consider the ODE given in (4). Let \(y_{c}\) (the complementary solution) denote the solution of the associated homogeneous equation

\begin{equation*} ay'' + by' + cy = 0 \end{equation*}

and let \(y_{p}\) (the particular solution) denote a single solution of (4). Then the general solution of (4) is given by \(y = y_{c} + y_{p}\text{.}\)
