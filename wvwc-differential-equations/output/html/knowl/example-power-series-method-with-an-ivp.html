Example 5.1.9. Power series method with an IVP.

Let \(y(x)\) denote the solution of
\begin{equation*} y'' + xy^\prime + y = 0, y(0) = 1, y^\prime(0) = 0\text{.} \end{equation*}
Find \(y\) up to the \(x^6\) term and determine the values of \(y''(0)\) and \(y^{(3)}(0)\text{.}\)
Solution.
It can be shown that \(y = e^{-x^2/2}\text{.}\)
