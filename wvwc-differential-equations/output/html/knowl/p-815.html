The solution of the heat equation (8.7) satisfying the boundary conditions (8.8) and initial condition (8.9) is given by
\begin{equation} u(x,t) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}b_{n}\sin\frac{n\pi}{L}x e^{-\lambda^{2}_{n}t}\tag{8.11} \end{equation}
where
\begin{align} b_{n} \amp = \frac{2}{L}\int_{0}^{L}f(x)\sin\frac{n\pi}{L}x\,dx \tag{8.12}\\ \lambda_{n} \amp = c\frac{n\pi}{L}. \tag{8.13} \end{align}
