We compute the Laplace transform \(F(s)\) using the definition:
\begin{align*} F(s) &= \int_{0}^{\infty}te^{-st}\,dt\\ &= \left[-\frac{t}{s}e^{-st}\right]_{0}^{\infty} + \frac{1}{s}\int_{0}^{\infty}e^{-st}\,dt &\text{using integration by parts}\\ &= -\frac{1}{s^{2}}\left[e^{-st}\right]_{0}^{\infty}\\ &= \frac{1}{s^{2}}\text{.} \end{align*}
