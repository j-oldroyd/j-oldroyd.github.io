Consider the first-order system
\begin{align} y^\prime_{1} \amp = a_{11}y_{1} + a_{12}y_{2}\tag{4.1}\\ y^\prime_{2} \amp = a_{21}y_{1}+ a_{22}y_{2}\notag \end{align}
or
\begin{equation*} \vec{y}^\prime = A\vec{y}\quad\text{where}\quad A = \begin{bmatrix}a_{11} \amp a_{12} \\ a_{21} \amp a_{22}\end{bmatrix}\text{.} \end{equation*}
