Let \(a,b\) and \(c\) be constants. Suppose that the characteristic equation of \(ay'' + by^\prime + cy = 0\) has distinct roots \(r_{1}\) and \(r_{2}\text{.}\) Then the general solution of the ODE is given by
\begin{equation*} c_{1}e^{r_{1}x} + c_{2}e^{r_{2}x}. \end{equation*}
