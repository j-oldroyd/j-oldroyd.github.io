Let \(f(x)\) be a periodic function with period \(2\pi\text{.}\) Then the Fourier coefficients of \(f(x)\) are given by
\begin{align*} a_{0} \amp= \frac{1}{2\pi}\int_{-\pi}^{\pi}f(x)\,dx\\ a_{n} \amp = \frac{1}{\pi}\int_{-\pi}^{\pi}f(x)\cos nx\,dx\text{ for $n\geq1$.}\\ b_{n} \amp= \frac{1}{\pi}\int_{-\pi}^{\pi}f(x)\sin nx\,dx\text{ for $n\geq 1$.} \end{align*}
