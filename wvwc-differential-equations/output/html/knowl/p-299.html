The systems of ODEs that we will consider will typically look like the following:

\begin{align*} x_{1}' &= a_{11}x_{1} +a_{12}x_{2}+\dots+a_{1n}x_{n}\\ x'_{2} &= a_{21}x_{1} + a_{22}x_{2} + \dots + a_{2n}x_{2}\\ &\vdots\\ x'_{n} &= a_{n1}x_{1} + a_{n2}x_{2} + \dots + a_{nn}x_{n} \end{align*}

where \(a_{ij}\) are constants and \(y_{i}\) are functions of \(t\text{.}\)
