Example 6.5.2. Convolution with Unit Step.

Compute \(u\ast t\text{,}\) where \(u(t)\) is the unit step function.
Solution.
By definition,
\begin{align*} u(t)\ast t &= \int_{0}^{t}u(\tau)(t-\tau)\,d\tau \\ &= \int_{0}^{t}(t-\tau)\,d\tau \\ &= \left[t\tau-\frac{\tau^{2}}{2}\right]_{\tau=0}^{\tau=t} \\ &= \frac{t^{2}}{2} \end{align*}
