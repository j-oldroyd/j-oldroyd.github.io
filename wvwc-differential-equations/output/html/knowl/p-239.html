Consider the ODE given in (2.3). Let \(y_{c}\) (the complementary solution) denote the solution of the associated homogeneous equation
\begin{equation*} ay'' + by^\prime + cy = 0 \end{equation*}
and let \(y_{p}\) (the particular solution) denote a single solution of (2.3). Then the general solution of (2.3) is given by \(y = y_{c} + y_{p}\text{.}\)
