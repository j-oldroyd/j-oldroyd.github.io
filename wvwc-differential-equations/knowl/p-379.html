Note that \(\vec{y}=\vec{0}\) is always a solution of \(\vec{y}'=A\vec{y}\text{.}\) This is because \(\vec{0}'=A\vec{0} = \vec{0}\text{.}\) We call \(\vec{y} = \vec{0}\) the equilibrium solution or critical point of the system \(\vec{y}'=A\vec{y}\text{.}\) In the next section, we will be concerned with the behavior of trajectories of the system \(\vec{y}'=A\vec{y}\) near the equilibrium solution. One thing we will see is that the behavior is determined in large part by the eigenvalues of the matrix \(A\text{.}\)
