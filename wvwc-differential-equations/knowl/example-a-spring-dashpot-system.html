Example 2.3.4. A Spring-Dashpot System.

Suppose that an object of mass \(m = 25\) is attached to both a spring and a dashpot. The mass is held \(1\) meter to the left of the spring's equilibrium position \(x = 0\text{.}\) The force of the spring on the mass is \(F_{S} = -226x\) and the force of the dashpot on the mass is \(F_{R} = -10x'\text{,}\) where \(x\) is the displacement of the mass. At time \(t = 0\text{,}\) the mass is released. Find \(x(t)\text{.}\)
