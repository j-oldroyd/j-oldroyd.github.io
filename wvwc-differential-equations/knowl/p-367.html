Just as we were able to plot direction fields for first-order ODEs, we can do something similar for first-order systems with two equations. Consider the first-order system

\begin{align} y'_{1} \amp = a_{11}y_{1} + a_{12}y_{2}\tag{4.2.1}\\ y'_{2} \amp = a_{21}y_{1}+ a_{22}y_{2}\notag \end{align}

or

\begin{equation*} \vec{y}' = A\vec{y}\quad\text{where}\quad A = \begin{bmatrix}a_{11} \amp a_{12} \\ a_{21} \amp a_{22}\end{bmatrix}. \end{equation*}
