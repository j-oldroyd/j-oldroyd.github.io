Theorem 6.4.1 Solution of the Heat Equation with Fixed Temperature

The solution of the heat equation (6.4.1) satisfying the boundary conditions (6.4.2) and initial condition (6.4.3) is given by

\begin{equation} u(x,t) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}b_{n}\sin\frac{n\pi}{L}x e^{-\lambda^{2}_{n}t}\tag{6.4.5} \end{equation}
where
\begin{align} b_{n} & = \frac{2}{L}\int_{0}^{L}f(x)\sin\frac{n\pi}{L}x\,dx \tag{6.4.6}\\ \lambda_{n} & = c\frac{n\pi}{L}. \tag{6.4.7} \end{align}
