Theorem 8.4.1. Solution of the Heat Equation with Fixed Temperature.

The solution of the heat equation (8.4.1) satisfying the boundary conditions (8.4.2) and initial condition (8.4.3) is given by

\begin{equation} u(x,t) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}b_{n}\sin\frac{n\pi}{L}x e^{-\lambda^{2}_{n}t}\tag{8.4.5} \end{equation}

where

\begin{align} b_{n} \amp = \frac{2}{L}\int_{0}^{L}f(x)\sin\frac{n\pi}{L}x\,dx \tag{8.4.6}\\ \lambda_{n} \amp = c\frac{n\pi}{L}. \tag{8.4.7} \end{align}
