Solution 6.1.4.1.

We compute the Laplace transform \(F(s)\) using the definition:

\begin{align*} F(s) \amp= \Int{0}{\infty}te^{-st}\,dt\\ \amp= \qty[-\frac{t}{s}e^{-st}]_{0}^{\infty} + \frac{1}{s}\Int{0}{\infty}e^{-st}\,dt \amp\text{using integration by parts}\\ \amp= -\frac{1}{s^{2}}\qty[e^{-st}]_{0}^{\infty}\\ \amp= \frac{1}{s^{2}}\text{.} \end{align*}
