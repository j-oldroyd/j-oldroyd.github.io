\begin{align*} y'_{1} \amp = ay_{1}-by_{1}y_{2}\\ y'_{2} \amp = ky_{1}y_{2} - ly_{2}\text{,} \end{align*}

where \(a,b,k,l>0\text{.}\) Find and classify the critical points of this system.
