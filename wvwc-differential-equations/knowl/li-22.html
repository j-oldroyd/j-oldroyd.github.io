Item 3

Find the general solution.

At this step it is easy to construct the solution of the matrix ODE. It's just

\begin{equation*} \vec{x} = c_{1}e^{\lambda_{1}t}\vec{v}_{1} + c_{2}e^{\lambda_{2}t}\vec{v}_{2} = \begin{bmatrix}c_{1}e^{-2it}(1-2i)+c_{2}e^{2it}(1+2i) \\ c_{1}e^{-2it}+c_{2}e^{2it}\end{bmatrix}. \end{equation*}
