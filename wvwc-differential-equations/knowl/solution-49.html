Solution 4.1.10.1

We need to find a matrix \(A\) and vector \(\vec{x}\) to let us rewrite this system. The matrix \(A\) is formed from the coefficients of \(x_{1},x_{2}\) on the right hand side of the system:

\begin{equation*} A = \begin{bmatrix}-\frac{1}{2}\amp 0\\\frac{1}{2}\amp -\frac{5}{7}\end{bmatrix}. \end{equation*}

The vector \(\vec{x}\) is just made up of the dependent variables \(x_{1},x_{2}\text{:}\)

\begin{equation*} \vec{x} = \begin{bmatrix}x_{1}\\x_{2}\end{bmatrix}. \end{equation*}

With these terms, the original system of ODEs is equivalent to the single matrix ODE

\begin{equation*} \vec{x}' = A\vec{x}. \end{equation*}
