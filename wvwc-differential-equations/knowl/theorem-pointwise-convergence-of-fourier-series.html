Theorem 7.1.7. Fourier Series of Piecewise Continuous Functions.

Let $f(x)$ be a piecewise continuous function on the interval $-\pi\leq x\leq\pi\text{,}$ and suppose that it's also periodic with period $2\pi\text{,}$ and is differentiable everywhere that it's continuous. Then the Fourier series of $f(x)$ converges to $f(x)$ except at the points where $f(x)$ is discontinuous.
