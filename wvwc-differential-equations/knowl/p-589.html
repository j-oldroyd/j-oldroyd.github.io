Let \(n\) be any integer. Then the functions \(\sin nx\) and \(\cos nx\) are both periodic and have period \(2\pi\) since

\begin{align*} \sin[n(x+2\pi)] \amp = \sin nx\cos2\pi + \cos nx\sin2\pi = \sin nx \\ \cos[n(x+2\pi)] \amp = \cos nx\cos2\pi - \sin nx\sin2\pi = \cos nx \end{align*}
