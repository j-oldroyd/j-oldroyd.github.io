This new ODE is separable, and so we separate variables and integrate to get \(\tan^{-1}u = x+C\text{.}\) If we don't care about finding an explicit solution, then we can just replace \(u\) to get the equation back in terms of \(y\text{.}\) So our (implicit) general solution is \(\tan^{-1}(x+y+3) = x+C\text{.}\)
