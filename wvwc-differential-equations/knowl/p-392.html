Let \(f(x)\) be a periodic function with period \(p = 2L\text{,}\) with corresponding Fourier series

\begin{equation*} a_{0}+\sum_{k=1}^{\infty}\left(a_{k}\cos\left(\frac{k\pi}{L}x\right)+b_{k}\sin\left(\frac{k\pi}{L}x\right)\right). \end{equation*}

If \(f(x)\) is an odd function, then \(a_{k} = 0\) for \(k\geq0\text{.}\) If \(f(x)\) is an even function, then \(b_{k}=0\) for \(k\geq1\text{.}\)
