Let \(I\) be some open interval\sidenote{We often take \(I\) to be \((-\infty,\infty)\text{,}\) but it doesn't have to be so.} and let \(\set{y_{1},\dots,y_{n}}\) be solutions of \cref{eqn:ode-constant-coeffs}. If \(W(y_{1},\dots,y_{n})\neq0\) for some point \(x_{0}\in I\text{,}\) then \(\set{y_{1},\dots,y_{n}}\) is linearly independent.
