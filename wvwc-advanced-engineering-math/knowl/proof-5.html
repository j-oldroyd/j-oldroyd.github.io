Proof

Define \(U = \smqty[\vb{u}_{1} \amp \ldots \amp \vb{u}_{n}]\text{.}\) Then \(U\) is orthogonal since its columns form an ONB by assumption. Now, note that

\begin{equation*} \sum_{i=1}^{n}\dotprod{\vb{x},\vb{u}_{i}}\vb{u}_{i} = U(U^{T}\vb{x})\text{.} \end{equation*}

Since \(U\) is orthogonal, this simplifies to \(I\vb{x} = \vb{x}\text{,}\) proving the theorem.
