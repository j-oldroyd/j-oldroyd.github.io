Definition 4.3.5. Flux Integral.

Let \(\FF = \mqty[P \amp Q]\) be a continuous vector field defined over a piecewise smooth curve \(C\) with outward facing normal vector \(\NN\text{.}\) The flux of \(\FF\) over \(C\) is defined to be

\begin{equation*} \int_C\FF\cdot\NN\dd{s} = \int_C P\dd{y} - Q\dd{x}\text{.} \end{equation*}
