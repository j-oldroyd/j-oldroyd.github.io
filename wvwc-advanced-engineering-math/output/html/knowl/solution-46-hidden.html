Such an ellipse can be given by the parameterization \(\rr(t) = \mqty[4\cos(t)\amp 2\sin(t)], 0\leq t\leq 2\pi\text{.}\) This gives

\begin{equation*} \frac{1}{2}\oint_C x\dd{y} - y\dd{x} = \frac{1}{2}\int_0^{2\pi} (8\cos^2(t) + 8\sin^2(t))\dd{t} = 16\pi \end{equation*}

for the area contained within the ellipse.
