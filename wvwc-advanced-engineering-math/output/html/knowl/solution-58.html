Solution 4.5.4.1.

To prove this we'll set \(\FF = f\del{g}\text{.}\) Then

\begin{equation*} \div\FF = \del{f}\cdot\del{g} + f\Delta g \end{equation*}

and

\begin{equation*} \FF\cdot\nn = f(\del{g}\cdot\nn) = f\pdv{g}{n}\text{,} \end{equation*}

and the Divergence Theorem then implies the result.
