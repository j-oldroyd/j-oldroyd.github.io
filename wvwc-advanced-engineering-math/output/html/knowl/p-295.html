For any nonzero \(\xx,\yy\in\RR^n\text{,}\) it follows that \(\frac{\dotprod{\xx,\yy}}{\norm{\xx}\norm{\yy}}\in[-1,1]\text{.}\) Values of this quotient close to \(1\) imply that the vectors are correlated whereas values close to \(-1\) imply that the vectors are anticorrelated. Values close to \(0\) imply that the vectors are nearly perpendicular.
