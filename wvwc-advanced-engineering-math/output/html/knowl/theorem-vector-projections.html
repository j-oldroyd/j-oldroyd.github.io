Theorem 3.1.2. Vector Projections.

Let \(\xx,\yy\in\RR^n\) with \(\yy\neq\vb{0}\text{.}\) The projection of \(\xx\) onto \(\yy\) is the vector \(\proj{\yy}{\xx}\) given by

\begin{equation*} \proj{\yy}{\xx} = \frac{\dotprod{\xx,\yy}}{\dotprod{\yy,\yy}}\yy\text{.} \end{equation*}
