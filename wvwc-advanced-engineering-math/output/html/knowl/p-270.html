Let \(\uu = \smqty[u_1 & u_2 & u_3]^T, \vv = \smqty[v_1 & v_2 & v_3]^T\in\RR^3\) and let \(\{\ii,\jj,\kk\}\) denote the standard basis vectors of \(\RR^3\text{.}\) Then

\begin{equation*} \uu\times\vv = \mqty| \ii & \jj & \kk \\ u_1 & u_2 & u_3 \\ v_1 & v_2 & v_3|\text{.} \end{equation*}
